Research Paper Draft - Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare

Abstract:
This paper examines the implementation of AI technologies in modern healthcare systems and their impact on patient outcomes.

Introduction:
Healthcare is undergoing a digital transformation with AI at the forefront. Machine learning algorithms are now being used for diagnostic imaging, drug discovery, and personalized treatment plans.

Key Findings:
- AI diagnostic tools show 94% accuracy in early cancer detection
- Automated prescription systems reduce medication errors by 78%
- Predictive analytics help hospitals reduce readmission rates by 23%

Methodology:
We analyzed data from 15 hospitals over a 2-year period, comparing traditional methods with AI-assisted processes.

Conclusion:
The integration of AI in healthcare demonstrates significant potential for improving patient care while reducing costs.
