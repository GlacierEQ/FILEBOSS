SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on March 15, 2024, between TechCorp Solutions LLC ("Company") and DataFlow Enterprises Inc. ("Client").

WHEREAS, Company provides software development and consulting services;
WHEREAS, Client requires custom software solutions for their business operations;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Company shall provide software development services including:
- Custom application development
- System integration services  
- Technical consulting and support
- Quality assurance testing

2. TERMS AND CONDITIONS
Term: 12 months commencing April 1, 2024
Payment: $50,000 monthly, due within 30 days of invoice
Confidentiality: Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality
Intellectual Property: All work product shall belong to Client

3. COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS
This agreement shall comply with:
- SOX regulations for financial reporting
- GDPR for data protection
- Industry standard security protocols

4. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate with 60 days written notice.
